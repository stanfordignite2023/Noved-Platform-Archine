    Photos & Social Media


    Stanford GSB Social Media Channels

    If you are sharing photos or connecting on social media, you can find the official @StanfordGSB pages at the following links: Stanford GSB LinkedIn | Executive Education LinkedIn | Stanford GSB Facebook | Stanford GSB Twitter | Stanford GSB Instagram

    Program hashtags include:
    #StanfordIgnite #StanfordGSB

    Program Google Photos Album

    We have created an official Google Photos Album for everyone to share photos taken throughout the Ignite program. Please remember to take lots of photos and upload them at https://photos.app.goo.gl/HL8yNiywxfwz1Saz9.

    Photo ideas include venture team selfies and group photos, social event photos, and all kinds of photos from academic sessions, team meetings, and program activities.

    Feel free to email your photos directly to gsb_ignite_full_time@stanford.edu if that is an easier option for you.

    Stanford Ignite Articles and Blog Posts

    If you are interested in writing up an article or blog post about your Stanford Ignite experience via LinkedIn, Wordpress, Medium.com, etc., feel free to use any staff-uploaded photos from our shared Google Photos Album and be sure to ask permission from your peers to use their shared photos. Please also remember to send the link to your post over to Ignite program staff at gsb_ignite_full_time@stanford.edu so we can share it with our Ignite alumni networks.

    For reference, feel free to take a look at one of our recent past-participant articles here.
